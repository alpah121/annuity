Annuity Design Page

Features

  • initial deposit minimum along with term length
  • invest in higher risk investments first
  • you can take a loan against it after one year from creation
  • gradually go to safer investments
  • stop payment when person dies
  • protected by 2FA
  • you don't have to make every payment
  • tells you if you are on track
  • formula for changing investment type: (max - min)/iterations
